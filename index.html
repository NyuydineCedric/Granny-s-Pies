Welcome to Granny's Pies

We've been baking pies the old-fashioned way for over 50 years. Our recipes have been passed down through generations, using only the finest ingredients and a whole lot of love. Whether you're a longtime fan or a first-time visitor, we're so glad you stopped by.

Browse our collection of classic pie recipes and discover why Granny's Pies has been a family favorite since 1972. Every crust is flaky, every filling bursts with honest flavor — just like the handwritten recipe cards from Granny's own kitchen.

About Us

Granny started baking pies in her small kitchen in rural Vermont back in 1972. What began as a hobby quickly turned into a passion — and then a business. Today, Granny's Pies ships across the country, but every recipe still follows the original handwritten cards from Granny's recipe box.

Our ingredients are sourced locally whenever possible, and we never use artificial preservatives. We believe great pie should taste like it was made just for you. Join our family table.
